        <h2>📈 Forecast Logic</h2>

<pre>
Revenue Forecast
= Previous Revenue × (1 + Revenue Growth)

COGS Forecast
= Previous COGS × (1 + COGS Growth)

Operating Expense Forecast
= Previous Opex × (1 + Opex Growth)

Gross Profit
= Revenue − Cost of Sales

Net Income
= Gross Profit − Operating Expense
</pre>
