<h1 align="center">📊 Financial Forecasting Model</h1>

<h2>📖 About</h2>

<p>
This project is a dynamic financial forecasting model developed in Microsoft Excel to predict future business performance. It uses growth assumptions to automatically forecast Revenue, Cost of Sales (COGS), Operating Expenses (Opex), Gross Profit, and Net Income.
</p>

<h2>🚀 Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Automatic Month-End Forecast Generation</li>
  <li>Revenue Forecasting</li>
  <li>COGS Forecasting</li>
  <li>Operating Expense Forecasting</li>
  <li>Gross Profit & Net Income Calculation</li>
  <li>Editable Growth Assumptions</li>
  <li>Dynamic Excel Formulas</li>
</ul>

<h2>📂 Project Structure</h2>

<ul>
  <li><b>Financial Modelling</b> – Actual financial data and monthly forecasts.</li>
  <li><b>Assumptions</b> – Revenue, COGS, and Opex growth rates.</li>
</ul>

<h2>📈 Forecast Logic</h2>

<pre>
Revenue Forecast = Previous Revenue × (1 + Revenue Growth)

COGS Forecast = Previous COGS × (1 + COGS Growth)

Opex Forecast = Previous Opex × (1 + Opex Growth)

Gross Profit = Revenue − Cost of Sales

Net Income = Gross Profit − Opex
</pre>
